# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, maintainers and
contributors of Dokimos pledge to make participation in the project a respectful,
harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful in language and tone.
- Assume good intent, but focus feedback on code and behavior.
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully.
- Share context, reproduction steps, and evidence when reporting issues.
- Prioritize the safety and privacy of users when discussing security-sensitive topics.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Deliberate disruption of discussions, reviews, or issue threads.
- Bad-faith reporting, spam, or abusive behavior toward maintainers or contributors.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of
conduct. They may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, or other
contributions that violate these standards.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainer via:

- <https://github.com/Thavarshan>

Please include relevant context, links, or screenshots where possible.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies within project spaces and in public spaces when an
individual is representing the project or its community.
